IIITB Student Kit is a guide for freshers which will help them when they get to IIITB. Basically, it has solutions to all the problems a student might face when they first visit the city. It has a list of hotels nearby, FAQs, list of documents required for completing the admission , everything about the life in IIITB , etc.

Visit IIITB Student Kit to check it out.
