Docker can be a difficult tool to get hold of initially. But once you get hold of it then it will change the way you do your work.

Docker right now is primarily used by large organizations for rapidly deploying applications at scale. Industry is quickly moving from the traditional monolithic architecture to modern distributed architectures. Instead of increasing the hardware capacity on a single system it is now more preferred to distribute the application on a pool of servers, indeed it comes with its own baggage of complications. You can't just migrate a traditional monolith app to a distributed application overnight but it is happening right now.

As students also docker can be a very useful tool for us. Especially if you are working on team projects and need to setup the development environment on multiple systems.
